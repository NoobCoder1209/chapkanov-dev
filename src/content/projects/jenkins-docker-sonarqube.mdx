A complete CI/CD playground that runs entirely on your machine: Jenkins for the
pipelines, SonarQube for the quality gates, and Docker for everything else.
The point isn't to ship anything to production — it's to show how the moving
pieces fit together when you can't lean on a managed platform.

The repo is mid-rebuild on the `rebuild/v1` branch. Final README, screenshots,
and quick-start instructions land in the last phase.
